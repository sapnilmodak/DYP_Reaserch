Advancing Science, Enhancing Lives: A Comprehensive Resource on Type 1 Diabetes

Keypoints

  • Pathophysiology: Loss of β-cell function, autoimmune responses (cellular, humoral).
  • Etiology: Genetic predisposition (HLA class II), environmental triggers.
  • Preclinical symptoms: Array of specified and generalized symptoms, duration variability, islet autoantibodies, insulitis prediction.
  • Metabolic indicators: Insulin release abnormalities, insulin resistance, T1DM prediction, insulin resistance.
  • Clinical trials: Halt autoimmunity, slow progression, preserve β-cell function.
